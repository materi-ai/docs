## Free Plan

Best for individuals and small teams exploring Materi.

**Includes:**

- Up to 5 team members
- 100 documents
- 1 GB storage
- 30-day version history
- 50 AI requests per month
- Real-time collaboration
- 2 integrations (Slack, GitHub)
- Community support

**Limitations:**

- Single workspace
- Basic templates only
- Read-only API access
- No SSO/SAML
- No audit logs

<Tip>
The Free plan has no time limit. Use it as long as you need while evaluating Materi.
</Tip>

**Everything in Free, plus:**

- Unlimited team members
- Unlimited documents

- 1-year version history
- 1,000 AI requests per month
- Full template library
- All integrations
- Full API access
- All export formats

- 99.9% uptime SLA
- Basic audit logs

**Pricing:**

| Billing | Price | Savings |
|---------|-------|---------|
| Monthly | $12/user/month | - |
| Annual | $10/user/month | 17% |

<Note>
Annual billing is charged upfront for the year. Monthly billing is charged at the start of each month.
</Note>

## AI Usage

AI features are included in all plans with usage limits:

| Plan | Monthly AI Requests | Overage |
|------|---------------------|---------|
| Free | 50 | Not available |
| Pro | 1,000 | $0.01/request |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom |

**What counts as an AI request:**

- Content generation (1 request per generation)
- Summarization (1 request per document)
- Code analysis (1 request per analysis)
- Enhancement suggestions (1 request per check)

<Tip>
Pro users can purchase additional AI credits in blocks of 1,000 requests for $8.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I try Pro before committing?">
    Yes! Start a 14-day free trial of Pro with no credit card required. You'll have access to all Pro features during the trial.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I hit the Free plan limits?">
    You'll see a notification but won't lose any data. Existing documents remain accessible, but you won't be able to create new ones until you upgrade or delete old documents.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I switch between plans?">
    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at your next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?">
    Yes! Nonprofits get 50% off Pro plans. Educational institutions can apply for free Pro access for students and educators. Contact us to learn more.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) for self-serve plans. Enterprise customers can pay by invoice with net-30 terms.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a minimum commitment for Enterprise?">
    Enterprise contracts are typically annual with a minimum of 50 seats. Contact sales for specific requirements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's included in the uptime SLA?">
    Pro plans include 99.9% uptime (approximately 8.7 hours downtime/year). Enterprise plans include 99.99% uptime (approximately 52 minutes downtime/year) with service credits for breaches.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my data if I cancel?">
    Yes, you can export all your content at any time, even after canceling. We provide export in Markdown, JSON, and PDF formats.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
